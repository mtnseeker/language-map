Bubble positions are approximate homeland/population centroids, not precise ranges. Migration routes are simplified great-circle paths summarizing scholarly reconstructions (Austronesian out-of-Taiwan; Bantu from the Nigeria–Cameroon borderlands; Indo-European Steppe hypothesis; etc.) — homeland claims range from well-supported to genuinely contested, as flagged. Family data: Wikipedia "List of language families" (Ethnologue/Glottolog). Map: D3 + Natural Earth land. Built as a static page — no servers, no tracking.
